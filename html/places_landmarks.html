The Great Court

Take a step back in time as you enter the monumental Great Court – the heart of The University of Queensland’s St Lucia campus. With its inviting lawn, historical buildings and quirky carvings, the Great Court has been a popular meeting place for decades.

UQ Lakes

UQ’s St Lucia Lakes have long been an iconic feature of the University grounds. They’re more than just a pretty face on campus — they irrigate the Great Court, St Lucia grounds and sporting fields, and are home to numerous native species of flora and fauna.

Advance Engineering Building

The Advanced Engineering Building provides an impressive range of educational facilities for engineering teaching and materials, manufacturing and civil engineering research. It incorporates multi-purpose active learning spaces where lectures are integrated with practical laboratory investigations.

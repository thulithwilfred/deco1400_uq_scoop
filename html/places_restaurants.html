Places to Eat


Patina Restaurant

Patina is a new fine dining restaurant that has recently opened up at UQ, in the Alumni court. Patina has a cafe/bar attached and a large indoor/outdoor venue with a capacity of upto 90 seats. Be sure to check them out for a premium experience!

Hours: Mon-Fri 7.00am - 5.30pm
Location: Patina Alumni Court

Physiol Refectory

Newly refurbished, the Physiol Refectory is a great place to find food at UQ. The building consists of a variety of smaller restaurants and cafes. Favourites like GYG, Kenko Sushi, Subway and many more. Physiol is a go to for many students and staff that look for variety of options.

Hours: Mon-Fri 7.00am - 5.30pm
Location: Physiol Refectory

Bagel Boys

UQ Bagel boys is placed behind the UQ Bioscience library, and is a great option for those looking for a range of bagel options and/or coffee.

Hours: Mon-Fri 7.00am - 11.00am
Location: UQ Bio Science Library

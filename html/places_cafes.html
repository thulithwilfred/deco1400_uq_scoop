Cafes at UQ


Merlo

Positioned next to the Duhig building, Merlo Cafe at UQ offers visitors a great cafe experience and a variety of options for coffee. They also have a variety of snacks and smaller meals to go with your coffee.

Hours: Mon-Fri 7.00am - 5.30pm
Location: Courtside, Duhig Building

Lakeside Cafe

The lakeside cafe is placed next to the Advanced Engineering building and has a great view of the UQ lakes. The Lakeside cafe is a family owned restaurant/cafe and they offer a range of authentic Vietnamese food and amazing coffee.

Hours: Mon-Fri 7.00am - 2.00pm
Location: Lakeside, Advanced Engineering Building

BrewPoint Cafe

BrewPoint at UQ, has a focus on sustainability, BrewPoint cafes source all materials with an eco-foot print in mind. They also offer discounts to customers with reusable cups. BrewPoint is a great choice if you are around the UQ Sports near the synthetic fields close to UQ center.

Hours: Mon-Fri 7.00am - 11.00am
Location: UQ Synthetic Fields, UQ Sports
